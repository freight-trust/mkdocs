ASC X12 004010

1139 Session Code
TYPE=ID MIN=1 MAX=1

Code identifying the type of academic session at an educational institution 


1        Full year
2        Semester
3        Trimester
4        Quarter
5        Quinmester
6        Mini-term
7        Summer Session
8        Intersession (Year Round Schools)
9        Long session which is longer than a semester or quarter or trimester, but 
         shorter than a full year
