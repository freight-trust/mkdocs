ASC X12 004010

1319 Basis of Cost Determination Code
TYPE=ID MIN=1 MAX=2

Code indicating the method by which the ingredient cost was calculated 


0        Not specified
1        Average Wholesale Price (AWP)
2        Local Wholesaler
3        Direct
4        Estimated Acquisition Cost
5        Acquisition Cost
6        Maximum Allowable Cost (MAC)
7        Usual, Customary, and Reasonable (UCR)
8        Unit Dose
9        Brand Medically Necessary
