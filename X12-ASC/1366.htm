ASC X12 004010

1366 Special Program Code
TYPE=ID MIN=2 MAX=3

Code indicating the Special Program under which the services rendered to the patient were performed 


01       Early & Periodic Screening, Diagnosis, and Treatment (EPSDT) or Child Health 
         Assessment Program (CHAP)
02       Physically Handicapped Children's Program
03       Special Federal Funding
04       Family Planning
05       Disability
06       Pneumococcal Pneumonia Vaccine (PPV) or Medicare 100% Payment
07       Induced Abortion - Danger to Life
08       Induced Abortion - Rape or Incest
09       Second Opinion or Surgery
10       Civilian Health and Medical Program of the Uniformed Services (CHAMPUS) 
         External Partnership Program
