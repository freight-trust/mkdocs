ASC X12 004010

1357 Prior Authorization Type Code
TYPE=ID MIN=1 MAX=1

Code indicating the type of prior authorization or medical certification that has occurred 


0        Not Specified
1        Prior Authorization
2        Medical Certification
3        Early & Periodic Screening, Diagnosis, and Treatment (EPSDT)
4        Exempt from Copay
5        Exempt From Prescription Limits
6        Family Planning
7        Aid to Families with Dependent Children (AFDC)
