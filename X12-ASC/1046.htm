ASC X12 004010

1046 Inlet or Outlet Fitting Type Code
TYPE=ID MIN=2 MAX=2

Code indicating the type of fitting required to make the connection of the inlet or outlet 


AC       Four Bolt Flange
DF       Dry Disconnect, Female
DM       Dry Disconnect, Male
QF       Quick Fit (Camlock), Female
QM       Quick Fit (Camlock), Male
TH       Threaded, Female
TM       Threaded, Male
