ASC X12 004010

1329 Dispense as Written Code
TYPE=ID MIN=1 MAX=1

Code indicating whether or not the prescriber's instructions regarding generic substitution were followed 


0        Not Dispense As Written (DAW)
1        Physician Dispense As Written (DAW)
2        Patient Dispense As Written (DAW)
3        Pharmacy Dispense As Written (DAW)
4        No generic available
5        Brand Dispensed as Generic
6        Override
7        Substitution Not Allowed-Brand Drug Mandated by Law
8        Substitution Allowed-Generic Drug Not Available in Marketplace
9        Other
